On 2022-09-15


It will soon be possible to explore the solar system from your home. This is indeed what NASA (National Aeronautics and Space Administration) has just announced by launching a partnership with Google, with the aim of reproducing our solar system in augmented reality (AR).


Google is already using AR technology for educational purposes, bringing many 3D models to life. The goal of this partnership is to expand its virtual offerings, by offering new and improved models of the celestial bodies around us, which are brought to life through augmented reality technology.

These animations are not only entertaining but also educational since they should offer historical anecdotes like the landing site of the Apollo 11 mission on the Moon.

So you will be able to find more than 60 planet models on Google Search from your browser, and no additional app is required to access historical annotations.


But this partnership is not limited to planetary models, you will indeed be able to explore comets, other planetary satellites than the Moon, as well as space objects created by Man (satellites, launchers, or even space telescope and Station Spatial).
